Wait... What is Jazz?

Jazz is a genre of music known for its funky beats, complex harmonies, syncopated rhythms, and —most importantly—an emphasis on improvisation.

Originating in New Orleans as a result of the slave trade, it took over the world for 35 years and now is known as one of the most popular genres that shaped the 1900s.
